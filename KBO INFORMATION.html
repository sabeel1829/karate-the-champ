About KBO

Karate Budokan Oman is a renowned Budokan style karate academy focused on discipline, self-defense, leadership, and physical fitness. The academy welcomes children, teens, and adults to learn karate professionally.


KBO provides indoor classes, outdoor training, competition opportunities, life skill programs, and advanced karate coaching.


"KARATE STARTS WITH RESPECT AND ENDS WITH RESPECT - OSS"
